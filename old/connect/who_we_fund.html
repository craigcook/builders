Crypto Founders

Cryptocurrencies enable a new level of economic freedom, access and control, decentralizing power and empowering people.

We are specifically excited about crypto ideas, projects and teams that are working on making Crypto easy to use for people who currently don’t know or care what crypto is.

In our team, we are focused on supporting entrepreneurs who are abstracting the complexity of crypto and focusing on the current consumer needs that can be more efficiently solved with cryptocurrencies.

Examples are:

  • Payments: p2p, international / local, between consumers / businesses / organizations / remittances, for merchants of any size.
  • Micropayments for content creators, as an alternative to advertising and beyond.
  • Onboarding, localized fiat-to-crypto solutions for every country in the world.
Browser

Browsers have been traditionally the window to the internet enabling the browser to keep information free and open. Mozilla has successfully built the first version of that browser - Firefox. We are excited to continue to support access to information via a browser. We are focused on supporting founders who are building new browser paradigms, better search solutions, or browser extensions that continue to keep the internet free and open.

If you are building the browser of the future, we would love to talk to you.

"Window to the Internet" Apps

Firefox has enabled a “window to the internet” and accessibility of information on the Desktop PC. However, on mobile the default access to the internet is through apps. Apps is the number one way people access the internet on the mobile phone. [1] We are looking for entrepreneurs who are building the next “window into the internet”. This can include apps or sites that shape our daily lives, where we get information whether it be friends or people we follow. Examples include apps or sites in the following areas: social media, information gathering, community, or even blogging / self expression.

If you are building a social app or any other “window to the internet” on the mobile phone, we would love to talk to you.
