Mozilla Builders was an experimental incubator program run throughout 2020. We invested in 80+ people, projects, and technologies reshaping the internet.

We learned there are founders and teams out there hungry to “fix the internet.” But they need support.

Mozilla is committed to working with people and projects like this. As a first step, we will launch Mozilla Ventures in early 2023 to support startups pushing the tech industry in a better direction. Additional programs are in the works.
